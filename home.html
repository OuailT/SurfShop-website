FUNTASTIC IMSOUANE

Imsouane is a small fishing village that we love! Far from everything but close to paradise…It is surrounded by an amazing nature that breathes peace and tranquility. It’s perfect to disconnect from stressful life and surf all day long! Surrounded by the ocean, Imsouane has an amazing surf potential within walking distance. The location offers a choice of 3 spots adapted to all levels.

Funtastic Imsouane is a surf shop and restaurant managed by its owner KARIM EL HJAJI 37 years old and from a Berber family from the city of Agadir who returns home after years of migration, to offer a wonderful atmosphere to his guests with creative ideas,He always dreamed of a "Surf Shop" store in which he combined services to teach surfing and drinks and delicious meals, which led him to choose the village of Imsouan, internationally known for this sport, before being attracted by the idea of cleaning the beach and its surroundings which
was inspired by childhood memories and instilled in the minds of his grandfather and grandmother.
“Smile before placing your order.” This is the phrase Karim placed at the front of the cafe, attracting the attention of passers-by and pioneers, to make them laugh or draw smiles emanating from the heart.

Everything you need to get you into the water in a matter of minutes, Rentals are a great option if you are new to the sport and want to try different types of surfboards, if you are only in Imsouane for a short time or if your own board isn’t ideal for the conditions of the day. We offer rentals for 3 hours so you can have fun and stay in the water for a little longer. We also offer daily and weekly rentals.
We rent surfboards and wetsuits individually as well as a combination deal
of a wetsuit and surfboard at a discounted price.we also have all surf gears you might need [ Leashes , Grips , Wax , Fins , ect...]

Overlooking one of the most famous surf spots on the Moroccan coastline and not only offering up a super healthy, fresh menu and serving amazing coffees, Café Funtastic Imsouane quickly becomes famous in Imsouane.
Whether you want an early breakfast, lunch, dinner or just a cup of tea and a slice of homemade cake, Café Funtastic Imsouane offers everything, from mega healthy smoothies to an epic veggie Moroccan Tagine!
Pop in, cozy up, enjoy a seriously good view with amazing music,and chow down on any of our tasty dishes at reasonable prices.
Café Funtastic Imsouane is open every day from 8 am to 11 pm non-stop. with Good vibes, all year round.
